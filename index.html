02 — Business Processes

End-to-End Process Flows

Core procurement and sourcing cycles mapped to SAP S/4HANA transactions and Fiori apps.

🛒

01

Purchase Requisition

Created manually or via MRP. Contains material, qty, delivery date, cost centre.

✅

02

PR Approval

Flexible workflow routes PR for approval based on value thresholds & org level.

🔍

03

Source of Supply

System proposes vendor via source list, info record, or contract.

📄

04

Purchase Order

PO created from PR or manually. Includes account assignment, delivery terms.

🚚

05

Goods Receipt

GR posted against PO. Inventory updated; GR/IR clearing account credited.

🧾

06

Invoice Verification

Vendor invoice verified via 3-way match. Variances trigger tolerance checks.

💳

07

Payment

FI posts payment run. Vendor account cleared. GR/IR reconciled.

📊

01

Spend Analysis

Identify sourcing opportunities using spend data and category analysis.

📁

02

Sourcing Project

Create sourcing project, define scope, timelines and team responsibilities.

📮

03

RFx Publication

Publish RFI/RFQ/RFP to selected suppliers with response deadline.

📥

04

Bid Collection

Supplier bids collected via portal. Buyer compares price & technical criteria.

⚖️

05

Evaluation & Award

Weighted scoring. Award decision made per lot or item level.

📋

06

Contract Creation

Contract auto-created from award. Terms, SLAs, and pricing embedded.

📝

01

RFx Creation

Buyer creates RFI, RFP, or RFQ with line items, questionnaires, and attachments.

👥

02

Supplier Selection

Invite registered or external suppliers. Define submission deadline & rules.

🔔

03

Publish & Notify

Suppliers receive notification. Collaborate on Q&A during open period.

📬

04

Bid Submission

Suppliers submit bids via SAP Business Network / Supplier Portal.

🏆

05

Compare & Award

Side-by-side bid comparison. Award complete, partial, or split across suppliers.

📩

01

Invoice Receipt

Invoice received via EDI, email, or manual entry in MIRO / Fiori.

🔗

02

PO Reference

Invoice matched to Purchase Order and Goods Receipt (3-way match).

⚠️

03

Tolerance Check

System checks price, quantity & delivery variances against tolerance limits.

🔒

04

Block / Park

Invoices with exceptions are blocked or parked. Buyer reviews & resolves.

✅

05

Release & Post

Approved invoices posted to FI. Vendor liability created for payment.
